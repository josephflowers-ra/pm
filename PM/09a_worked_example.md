### 12. Lessons Learned

**What went well**
- Partnering with schools drove nearly half of all volunteer signups.
- The one-page charter made stakeholder expectations explicit and reduced "but I thought…" moments.
- RAG status in the weekly update triggered the social-media-boost decision before the shortfall got serious.

**What did not go well**
- Recruitment started one week too late. Signups only accelerated after week 2.
- Two volunteers arrived without the safety brief; check-in flow missed them.
- Recycling vendor did not confirm pickup until 24 hrs before the event, creating unnecessary stress.

**What to improve next time**
- Start recruitment in week 0, not week 1.
- Make the safety brief a gate at check-in (no vest without it).
- Require vendor confirmation by the milestone date, not the day before.

**Actions assigned**
- Save the signup page and flyer templates in the shared folder. *(Alex, Apr 5)*
- Add "safety brief gate" to the next charter's day-of plan. *(Jamie, next kickoff)*
- Add vendor confirmation milestone to default project plan. *(Alex, before next project)*
